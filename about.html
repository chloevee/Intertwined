Intertwined is a collective that aims to shed light on the realities of digital courtship in the Philippines. By exploring how dating apps shape the way Filipino youth experience romance and relationships, we hope to spark meaningful conversations and not only inform, but also encourage self-reflection and awareness among the youth and their parents.

MISSION

Our mission is to empower Filipino youth and their parents to understand, reflect on, and navigate digital courtship in a safe and healthy environment.

VISION

Our vision is to create a community that navigates digital courtship with empathy, understanding, and cultural awareness.
