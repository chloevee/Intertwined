INTERTWINED

The Intertwined Project is a collective that aims to shed light on the realities of digital courtship in the Philippines. By exploring how dating apps shape the way Filipino youth experience romance and relationships, we hope to spark meaningful conversations and not only inform, but also encourage self-reflection and awareness among the youth and their parents.

We chose to explore the topic of digital courtship because dating apps have become a big part of how Filipino youth experience romance today. Many young people and their parents face emotional challenges, cultural stigma, and misunderstandings around online dating. Our goal is to shed light on these experiences, promote emotional awareness, and bridge the gap between traditional Filipino values and modern relationships.

The website is mainly designed for Filipino youth aged 18-24 who are exploring or have experienced digital courtship as well as for parents, educators, or anyone interested in understanding how technology and the digital age is shaping modern Filipino romance.

Our content is based on research findings, surveys, interviews, and credible sources related to Filipino digital courtship.

You can help by sharing our website with friends, family, or peers; starting conversations about healthy digital courtship; and engaging with our interactive content. You are also free to support us by answering our survey here to get more feedback on how we can further improve this project!
